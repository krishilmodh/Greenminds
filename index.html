About Us

Our website aims to bridge the gap the individuals who want to clean the area/ remove wastes and the organizations looking for cleaning in order to make a clean environment. We want this process to be hassle free so that each one of us can donate to our full extent easily. Now all you need is a good heart to take up the step towards cleanliness which we know you already have because all the other things will be taken care by us. The problem was clearly evident that most of the garbage dumps/site we see, we want to clean it but are not able to do it alone. The solution to this problem is also that simple. If you see any garbage dump site, visit our website and make a contribution. Please enter your contact details and the place specifications. After that enter your location, as soon as you enter your location you can see all the active organisations around you, their volunteers can come and get your area clean really soon. Then you can track the volunteer which will come. YAY you just made a change.

How We Work

Network

We all want to help reduce garbage but we don't know what to do and who to contact. Here's where our website comes into the picture.

Step 1

Let's say you came across some really awful garbage dump site in our way in the city. You want to clean this filth but it seems like an hassle doing it alone. Here's how we can make this simple. All you have to do is visit this website. Look for the nearest active organization around you.

Step 2

So now you have found the nearest active non-for profit organization around you, just one more step and your work is done. Just click on the contribution button and enter your contact details and the place specifications.

Almost Done

Now just sit back because now we will make sure that someone from the selected organization reaches your location in 40 mins to clean up the site as soon as possible.YAY, you just made a difference. One step towards the success of swatch bharat abhiyan. Feels good, right!!!!
